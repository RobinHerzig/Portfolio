Robin Herzig

Tech and helping others are my passions.

After graduating, I found fullfillment in working as a 911 dispatcher. After several years, I accepted an amazing opportunity to with the Antarctica Fire Department in Mcmurdo Research Station, Antarctica. This would spark my interest to become a software engineer.

Frustrated by the lack of an adequate CAD (computer-aided dispatch) program, I created my own. First with Microsoft Word, using content controls to add functionality. Then, after several iterations, I studied JavaScript to add even more features.

After several revisions, I'm happy to release my CAD software so that it may help agencies who cannot afford their own. Furthermore, I'm excited to continue my journey as a software engineer by taking on new projects and solving new problems.
