Robin Herzig - Software Engineer

Several years ago, I accepted an amazing opportunity to work in Mcmurdo Research Station, Antarctica with the Antarctica Fire Department.

Frustrated by the lack of adequate CAD (computer-aided dispatch) software, I sought to create my own. After several revisions, I'm happy to release First Line Response so that it may help agencies who cannot afford their own. Furthermore, I'm excited to continue my journey as a software engineer by taking on new projects and solving new problems.

I invite you to explore First Line Response by visiting the official website or Github repo.
